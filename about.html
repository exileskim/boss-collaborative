Annemarie's Story

For over a decade, I have been designing and facilitating collaborative experiences for organizations navigating their most complex challenges. My work spans the intersection of science, government, education, and industry — from aligning hundreds of NASA researchers around shared objectives to reimagining community programs at the Dali Museum.

I founded Boss Collaborative on a core belief: that the biggest breakthroughs happen when diverse perspectives are brought together with intention, structure, and creative energy. Too often, organizations default to conventional meetings and top-down decision-making when what they actually need is facilitated collaboration — a structured space where every voice contributes to a shared outcome.

My approach draws from design thinking, systems theory, and creative facilitation methodologies. Whether I'm working with a team of 10 or a conference of 500+, the goal is the same: move people from complexity to clarity, from silos to shared understanding, and from ideas to action.

I believe our most complex challenges deserve more than a meeting. They deserve a designed experience that draws out collective wisdom and turns it into forward motion. Annemarie Boss
